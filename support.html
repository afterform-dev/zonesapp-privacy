Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a city?

Tap the "+" button at the bottom of the main screen and search for any city. The app includes over 9,000 cities and works offline.

How do I compare time across cities?

Drag the time picker at the bottom of the main screen. You'll see how the time changes in all your cities as you drag.

How do reminders work?

Reminders are tied to specific cities. Set a reminder for 9:00 AM in Tokyo, and you'll be notified at the right time in your local time zone.

How do I enable iCloud Sync?

Go to Settings in the app and turn on "iCloud Sync". Your cities and reminders will sync across all your devices signed into the same iCloud account.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings, tap on "Subscription", and then tap "Restore" in the top right corner of the screen.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to iPhone Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions → Zones to manage or cancel.
